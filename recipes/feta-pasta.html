Baked Feta Pasta

A Family Favourite: Quick, Easy, and Delicious

  • 500 g ripe cherry OR grape tomatoes
  • 8 garlic cloves
  • ~200 g of feta cheese
  • 400 g large shell pasta
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil (additional if requried)
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • handful of fresh basil leaves (not required)
  • salt and pepper to taste
  1. Preheat the oven to 200°C/400°F.
  2. Add cherry tomatoes + garlic cloves (with skins) to an oven dish.
  3. Season with oregano, salt, and pepper. Pour over olive oil. Mix together.
  4. Place block of feta cheese in middle of the dish. Sprinkle more oregano and drizzle olive oil over the block of feta.
  5. Bake for 25-30 minutes (until tomatoes blistered + feta cheeese softens).
  6. When tomatoes have 10 minutes left on the cooking time, start to cook the pasta in heavily salted water.
  7. Take oven dish out. Peel garlic cloves. Mash everything together into a sauce-like consistency.
  8. Add cooked pasta into dish. Mix well. Add pasta water if required.
